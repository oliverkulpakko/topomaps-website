1. Bookmarks

How can I add a place to bookmarks?

You can add a bookmark by holding your finger on the map at the desired location.

You will be prompted to give a name for the new bookmark. It is not required, but helpful if you have a lot of bookmarks.

Where can I find my bookmarks?

Tap the bookmarks icon (a book) in the bottom center of the screen.

Tap a bookmark in the list to open it on the map.

I have an iPhone and an iPad, and I'd like to have the same bookmarks on both devices. Is it possible?

When you are logged in to an account, all your bookmarks will be automatically synced between your devices.

How can I delete a bookmark?

Go to the bookmark list, and swipe from right on the bookmark. A red "Delete" button will appear.

How can I import a GPX file?

When tapping a GPX file in an another app (e.g. Files, Mail), there will be list of available apps. Tap Topo Maps on the list, and you'll be able to import the GPX-file.

My bookmarks were lost after I updated the app?

In some cases, updating to version 6.0 deleted all bookmarks. Tapping this link: topo-maps://migrate-icloud. After a successful restore, there will be an alert with a text "Migration Complete". Please contact us if you have any questions..

2. Map Layers

How can I change the map layer?

You can change the map layer by going to the app's settings and selecting Map Layer.

How does the map cache work?

As you're browsing the map, everything that you see on your screen is automatically stored.

You can clear the cache by going to the app's settings and selecting Clear Cache.

Map Downloader can also be used to download maps from an area.

3. Map Downloader

How does the Map Downloader work?

You can launch the map downloader by going to the app's settings, and selecting Map Downloader.

After that, zoom the map to the area you want to download and tap the Start-button. One download's size is limited to 100km (or 60 mi). Please keep in mind that larger areas can take a considerable amount of time to download.

Previously downloaded areas will be shown in yellow when selecting an area. It does not matter if a new download area overlaps with a previously downloaded area. Overlaps will automatically be ignored.

You can delete downloaded areas by going to the app's settings and selecting Clear Caches.

Can I download maps from any map layer?

Due to licensing issues, all map layers are not able to be downloaded.

List of maps where downloading is not enabled:

  • Thunderforest Outdoors
  • Topo Switzerland (swisstopo)

4. User Account

What features does the user account offer?

When logged in, you can share your location with other users, and your bookmarks will be synced between your devices.

How can I create a user account?

You can create an account by going to the app's settings, tapping Log In, and after that select Create Account.

I've forgotten my password.

Tap the Forgot Password?-button in the login screen. Enter your email address. If there is an account with the provided email address, a message with instructions to reset your password will be sent.

If the email is incorrect, or there is no account with that email address, nothing will be sent.

I'd like to delete my account or check the data that's stored about me.

Send us an email and we'll help.

5. Location Sharing

What is location sharing?

Location Sharing allows you to share your location live with other users of the app.

How can I share my location with another user?

  1. Make sure that both of you are running the latest version of the app, and have created own accounts.
  2. Go to the app's settings on your device
  3. Tap your account
  4. Tap Location Sharing
  5. Tap the plus-icon in the top right corner
  6. Enter the other user's email address
  7. Optionally, you can enter a custom expiration date for the share. It's one year by default.
  8. Now your location will be shown on the other user's devices.

When you are creating a location share, you are only sharing the current device's location. For example, if you share your location on your iPhone, your iPad's location won't be shared.

You can share your location with as many users as you like.

You can delete the share even before the expiration date by tapping on the share and tapping the trash can.

How often is my location updated?

The location is updated every 10 seconds while the app is active.

6. Haku

Miten pääsen hakuun?

Pääset hakuun painamalla hakupalkkia kartan yläreunassa.

Mitä voin hakea?

Haku-toiminnon avulla voit hakea osoitteita, paikkoja ja koordinaatteja. Voit myös liittää tekstiä hakukenttään.

Miten koordinaattien haku toimii?

Sovellus tunnistaa, jos olet kirjoittanut numeroita kenttään. Silloin sinulle ehdotetaan mahdollisuutta avata hakusana koordinaatteina.

7. Ota yhteyttä

Jäikö jotakin epäselväksi, vai tarvitsetko apua sovelluksen kanssa?

Ota yhteyttä laittamalla sähköpostia osoitteeseen tuki@topomaps.app. Vastaan yleensä jo saman päivän aikana.

Jos sinulla on ongelma sovelluksen kanssa, kannattaa viesti lähettää suoraan sovelluksen asetusten kautta, niin tuki saa suoraan kaikki tarvittavat tiedot.
