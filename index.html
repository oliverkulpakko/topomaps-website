Topo Maps

Topo Maps is a mapping application for iPhone and iPad. It includes topographic maps to 15 countries. You can preview different map types here.

Download on the App Store

Features:

  1. Maps

    All of the maps are the sharpest and the newest ones available.

    Maps are periodically updated.

  2. Map Downloader

    Map Downloader allows you to download maps from your favorite areas for offline usage. You can download as many areas as you want.

  3. Bookmarks

    Add your own waypoints to the map just by holding your finger. You can also record routes and save them too. And if you have multiple devices, the bookmarks are automatically synced between them via iCloud.

  4. Location Sharing

    Share your location with other Topo Maps users by giving them your Location Sharing Code. They will be able to see you on their map when you have your app opened. You can share your location to as many people as you like. You can also add as many people to your map as you want.

  5. Search

    Search for places, addresses or coordinates. A list of suggestions based on your current location will be displayed.
